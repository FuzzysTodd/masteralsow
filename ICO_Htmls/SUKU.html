purpose

our role in this market shift

helping companies better serve conscious consumers

Large corporations and brands don’t yet know how to address and provide the transparency the conscious consumer wants.

helping consumers find truthful, transparent, verified products

Nearly 40% of customers say they would switch from their current preferred brand to one that offers more transparency.

Value Proposition

enabling the future of commerce

consumer demand

$1T untapped market

SUKU aims to help brands satisfy the 21% of consumers who do not purchase sustainably due to lack of verification, quality, performance, and high costs.

product differentiation

efficiency & transparency

Increased brand transparency, coupled with supply chain efficiency, enables brands to differentiate products and verify product claims.

new business opportunities

connected network

suku Scanner

a powerful verification tool

Our solution traces products from raw materials to finished goods, allowing brands to offer differentiated, transparent products with attributes that consumers can view and verify through the SUKU blockchain.

suku platform

backed by our supply chain ecosystem

SUKU’s blockchain-based supply chain ecosystem enables retailers and suppliers to transact efficiently, securely, and transparently. Product traceability is among the many other efficiencies created through this interaction on the ecosystem which are then translated to the end consumer.
