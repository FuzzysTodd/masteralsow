Equity for everyone

Equity has often been out of reach for large portions of the population without many years of savings or a rich uncle. Renters in a Rise building can build equity without the massive barrier to entry. This equity is portable; renters can easily move their tokens to a different RISE property when they want to move.

Owners don’t always have it easy either. They are often trapped in many years of high payments reducing their quality of life. Partnering with Rise, they now have the option of gaining instant liquidity while providing better options for their tenants.

Global Asset Class

Our platform allows tenants and everyday participants to be able to buy equity in real estate, one token at a time, without any lending or interest rates involved.
