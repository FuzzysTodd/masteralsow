    2014 - 2016 - CHALK Experiment

    A GIVE-alike token called Girim (Chalk in hebrew) was launched in 2014 and adopted by 150,000 students from over 80 schools, supported by the Ministry of Education of Israel. With 72 nonprofits, advertisers and retailers taking part in the ecosystem, the experiment saw some amazing result including a wealth increase amongst the students.
