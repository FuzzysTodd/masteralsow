Functionalities
to move up a gear

Automatic import of all bank transactions

Link your bank account with your accounting procedures so that all your bank transactions are automatically reported in EZYcount. No need to manually copy data and above all else, no more data entry mistakes.

Simple and self-explanatory wording

Instead of debit and credit, let's refer to inflow and outflow. Now you're set to master EZYcount quickly. Need another example? In EZYcount, bookings are in colors. Between green and red, it's easy to figure out which one is an outflow isn't it?

Professional legal reports

Create the necessary reports for your tax, bank and other legal entities. Thanks to EZYcount, the reports are clear, professional and follow the structure laid out by financial experts. Project a professional image of your activity with EZYcount

Collaboration

Work together online and directly with your fiduciary. Forget sending accounting and other sensitive information through insecure mediums such as emails and USB sticks.
