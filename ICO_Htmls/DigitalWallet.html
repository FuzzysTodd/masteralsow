SMILES Mobile Remittance

SMILES is a mobile wallet service platform under Digital Wallet Corporation. The service provides a money transfer service using a smartphone based application. This application has been offering real-time, low-cost, and 24-hour availability service based on our original platform.

Fair Wallet Service for International Citizens

The world has become and entered into an age where human resources move and cooperate with each other across the world due to the remarkable evolution of transportation methods, improvement of communication infrastructure, and human resources through exchanged policies and treaties. Digital Wallet Group has already established a Fintech based mobile wallet service in Japan that supports the life of International Citizens. But to further expand the service in each country, users can easily invest using crypto currency.  We would like to provide such financial services that can be done.  We hereby propose to you the Digital Wallet Project. This project is based on an existing and already profitable service by Digital Wallet Corporation (Japan). Furthermore, we strive to make crypto currency a more universal and continuous financial platform through Digital Wallet Project.

Project Details

Project Purpose

Establish a Brand New Financial Service Platform for International Citizens

As a first step, we have built a platform that users can send money directly to other countries at any time 24 hours a day and 7 days a week through your mobile phones.  That mobile remittance service “ Smiles ” has earned the highest number of downloads ever in the Japan market and  the business income has been profitable already.  Based on this platform, we plan to expand the service to Asian countries to start a new investment service.  Digital Wallet Project plans to expand in the  following three areas.

Digital Wallet Token

Issue Digital Wallet Token (DWC) based on ERC20 format.  Not only will we develop services in each country, but we will introduce new services by abiding to local laws and regulations.

Deployment of Wallet Service

Expand our wallet services platform such as mobile money transfer to Asian countries besides Japan.

Investment Service

Invest in early stage development projects, agricultural projects and the likes in major Asian countries making such investment services available to International Citizens.

Mobile Remittance Service Smiles

Our platform directly allows remittance to the Philippines, Vietnam, Indonesia, and etc.; hence, not through the existing banking infrastructure.  By the second quarter of 2016, our service had already begun in Japan. As a mobile money transfer service “ Smiles ,” the service provides a money transfer service using a smartphone based application.  This application has been offering real-time, low-cost, and 24-hour availability service based on our original platform.  After launching this service, as a Mobile Money Transfer Service, it has become the largest number of downloaded application in Japan.  Business revenue has been profitable even before the estimated target plan for the company.  Digital Wallet Corporation (Japan), which operates “Smiles,” has reached and achieved Net Sales of 3 million USD with Operating Profit Ration of 11.0% in 2018.

Digital Wallet Project

Mobile Wallet

We aim to become a digital platform for International Citizens’ international money transfer by promoting cashless payment directly from the wallet.  By 2019-2020, we plan to expand to several Asian countries including the Philippines and also to  Europe as a Remittance Source. We already acquired a Philippine Fintech company on October 2019 and continue to take progress towards our goals.

Investment Service

In 2019-2020, we plan to merge the investment in the emerging boomtown such as developing projects in the surrounding areas where new airport development is scheduled to take place; expanding in the agricultural investment areas where productivity improvement investment operates in several Asian countries, mainly in the Philippines.

Company & Team

Digital Wallet was set up in 2014 by the Founder & President, Eiji Miyakawa, who had been engaged for many years at Sony; basically, to develop and operate Mobile Payments, electronic money, contactless IC technology and cloud technology thereby aiming to create a direct Wallet network between individuals in mobile age.  Under the direction of the Founder, who is qualified also as lawyer and consequently has a thorough knowledge of rules, regulations and laws about the new service.  Our company has succeeded to launch the services for International Citizens.  We have become a successful Fintech Company that has strength in both technology and regulations of law with an excellent engineering team, who had worked together with the founder of Sony, developing business mainly in Japan but also in Manila, Philippines, in Ho-Chi-Min, Vietnam, and in Tallinn, Estonia.
