SmartRefer: The First-Ever Blockchain Referral and Recruiting Marketplace

Happy holidays to our dear clients! Smart Refer Team welcomes you at our Medium page. We've decided to take a fresh start this year and develop our team's openness by updating Medium and posting new articles every week.

In SmartRefer, we hold the view that a person is best known by familiars, relatives, and comrades. And, perhaps, Google.

Reliance on your personal connections while searching for fresh employment choices allows you to find more suitable positions in the best companies available.

SmartRefer provides a marketplace-type service, targeted at people on the platform referring each other. Its aim is to use modern technologies, such as machine learning and blockchain, to assist candidates in finding open jobs in IT-related industries.

Through SmartRefer, any individual is capable of recommending a friend for a position in a certain company. Users whose suggested friends were employed by organizations would later receive rewards in the form of a currency named Fiat combined with special ERC-223 REFER Membership tokens. The above benefits are later to be exchanged on our “SmartMarket” for certificates, vouchers, successful employment suggestions, CV filling tips, and job skill upgrades or, upon your choice, for goods and services offered by other organizations through the SmartRefer website.

SmartRefer is capable of beating all current sources of locating and hiring talents in IT-related fields.
