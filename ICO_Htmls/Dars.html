What is DARS

DARS is the world's first blockchain platform for referral marketing and traditional business that provides remuneration payments to partners and users as well as stability of conditions through smart contracts and allows creating the remuneration compensation plans of any complexity.

DARS is not just a platform, it is a whole ecosystem that includes an internal exchange, educational platform, association for protection of companies' and partners' rights.

Problems that are solved by the DARS blockchain

After the DARS launch, not a single partner in the whole world will ever return to the classic recommendation systems

Current issues

Financial frauds

The presence of frauds that put on a mask of an honest business. The risk of being deceived and, as a consequence, a checkered reputation of the referral programs

Industry in isolation

A checkered reputation of the referral programs, a danger of financial losses and lack of technology scares customers, partners and investors from a quite successful industry.

Violation of partners' rights

Non-payment of remunerations, modification of the compensation plan terms, abuse of power by the company's presidents, including exclusion of partners from the network

Creation of a compensation plan is long and expensive

Expensive and complex software; it takes months to implement it. Dependence on unreliable developers and potential system security problems

DARS solutions

Full transparency

The own DARS blockchain ensures transparency and stability of all financial transactions, which excludes fraud

Reputation enhancement

Reputation enhancement through process automation and development of an internal ecosystem that includes an educational platform and association for protection of companies' and partners' rights

Performance guarantee

A smart contract technology ensures for the partners automatic fulfilment of all obligations within the system

Easy, reliable and almost for free

DARS is the world's first blockchain platform for referral marketing and traditional business on blockchain that allows creating systems of any complexity, not depending on the developers afterwards
