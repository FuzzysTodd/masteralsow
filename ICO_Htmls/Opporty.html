Our expertise

Smart Contract Development
A smart contract is a blockchain protocol that acts as an electronic agreement and prescribes specific actions to be executed, once the defined conditions are met.
Dapps Development
Decentralized applications are apps underpinned by smart contracts. Full autonomy and advanced security make them superior to traditional apps.
Private Blockchain
Enterprises that do not want to expose sensitive data in a public blockchain prefer to utilize a private blockchain. There are multiple ways of achieving privacy with Blockchain. Our professionals will help you select the best options, based on your expectations and business needs.
Crypto Payment Processing
Do you plan to use cryptocurrency for business transactions? Then you need a fast and fault-tolerant crypto payment processing application. We are here to develop it for you!
Crypto Wallet Development
Crypto wallet is a type of software that enables you to send and receive cryptocurrencies. It provides an open and private key to ensure 100% protected use of a crypto wallet by its owner.
Blockchain Consulting
Are you new to blockchain and want to leverage this remarkable innovation? Schedule a personal consultation with our dedicated blockchain specialists. In the course of a friendly conversation, we will pinpoint the strengths and weaknesses of your business, and select the right tech tools to accelerate its success.
Exchange Platforms
Join the mass revolution of blockchain adoption and launch a cryptocurrency exchange platform within your organization to convert digital currency into traditional money or other assets.
Etherium Development
Ethereum is a platform for creating decentralized applications based on blockchain, a distributed ledger operating through the use of smart contracts.
ICO Development and Launch
An Initial Coin Offering is a great way to release new utility token/currency and collect funds for your newly launched startup. ICOs are subject to prohibition and regulations in some countries, so check to see if they are allowed in your region.
STO Development and Launch
Create a Security Token Offering to manage your physical assets, presented in digital form. Your virtual deals will be backed by real values, and information about the ownership of values will be recorded in tokens.
IEO Development and Launch
An Initial Exchange Offering is a way to raise funds for blockchain projects (startups) by placing tokens on cryptocurrency exchanges. IEOs are subject to prohibition and regulations in some countries, so check to see if they are allowed in your region.
