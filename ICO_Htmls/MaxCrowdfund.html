WHY BUY MPG?

1 Diminishing supply

Transaction costs are charged in Euros when a registered user makes an investment on Max Crowdfund. The fees earned are used to buy MPG tokens on exchanges.

The purchased MPG tokens fee will be burned, thus the supply will always be diminishing.

2 Increase in value

One could argue that the MPG token value can only increase as the supply will always be diminishing. We will continue to buy MPG tokens on exchanges until the supply runs out.

No guarantees are provided and making investments always carry risks.

3 Trading on exchanges

Just like equity investors are trading on stock exchanges, people can also trade on crypto exchanges. There can be great opportunities when investing in the right projects.

Remember, be cautious and always do your own research prior to making an investment.

HOW TO BUY MPG UTILITY TOKENS?

CoinMarketCap.com

MPG tokens are traded on various exchanges, as you can see on CoinMarketCap.com . You will be able to see the price per MPG and the trading volume on each exchange, which should enable you to choose where you would like to buy MPG tokens.

Crypto Exchanges

Crypto exchanges are like stock exchanges, such as the NYSE , LSE , Euronext , AEX , etc. in the sense that crypto tokens are traded there. However, unlike on stock exchanges, where you need a broker to trade, you can have a personal account on crypto exchanges.

Buying MPG tokens

You will need to create an account on the exchange where you want to buy MPG tokens. Most exchanges require you to complete the Know Your Client (KYC) process to have an approved account, before you are able to buy and trade MPG tokens.

MPGS SECURITY TOKEN DETAILS

MPGS tokens are Security Tokens, which represent one Max Property Group Share Certificate. Max Property Group B.V. has issued shares to Max Property Coöperatie U.A., which in turn has issued Max Property Group Share Certificates. Each Share Certificate is represented by one MPGS Security Token. MPGS have the same rights to profit share and voting rights as the normal shares of Max Property Group B.V. All investment documents and due diligence documents regarding the MPGS Security Tokens can be found in the MPGS Asset page on this website.
