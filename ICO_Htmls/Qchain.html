Authentic Marketing Means Engaged Consumers

“Authentic Marketing” describes our focus on branded content, influencer marketing, and sponsorships.



Branded Content



Influencer Marketing



Sponsorships



The public’s attitudes towards digital advertising are shifting. In 2017, the use of ad-blocking software rose by 30% worldwide. Consumers are turned off by traditional display ads and are seeking (and responding to) more personalized and informative experiences from brands. The three areas that Qchain is focusing on share a common thread of bringing these engaging advertorials to consumers. Branded content refers to advertisers paying digital publishers to create custom content: videos, written articles, and other forms of storytelling, that are native to the publishers’ sites. Influencer marketing, meanwhile, focuses on a different form of social sharing: It taps into the influence that specific individuals have over their fanbases. Advertisers will pay these individuals for endorsements on their social channels. And finally, sponsorships, wherein advertisers offer financial support to an individual, event, or activity, in exchange for visibility — e.g. a sportswear brands sponsoring an athlete, who will then wear that brand’s products while competing. The demand for authentic marketing is growing, and for understandable reasons: It’s both more effective and ethical than traditional display advertising. Publishers, advertisers, and viewers are more closely connected and more engaged, making for a more mutually beneficial marketing experience. Consequently, these forms of advertising place less emphasis on less savory industry practices, like data mining, fingerprinting, and targeting.

Our Platform is a One-Stop Shop for Content Campaigns

Our platform will enable easy and flexible transactions between content creators and advertisers. Content creators — whether they are digital outlets or individual bloggers — will have great range in selecting the types of branded content they are able to build: written articles, podcasts , short films, interpretive dance performances… You name it. Influencers will be able to place listings for vocal endorsements during streams or social posts across a variety of social channels. Similarly, talent agents seeking sponsorships for their clients will also be able to place listings on the platform. To facilitate the creation of the content itself, we are building document and collaboration tools that allow content creators to manage their projects post-sale. Our vision is to seamlessly integrate the content purchasing and content creation process, making our platform a one-stop shop for content campaigns.

A Blockchain-Agnostic Development Approach

The Custom Content Marketplace backend will be developed to easily interface with any blockchain that has a corresponding RESTful API. We have chosen to orient our platform towards NEM and Ethereum presently, because those are two blockchains that we feel have the most enterprise potential and have robust community and developer support. However, we are primed for a future in which there may be multiple competing and successful blockchain technologies.

Tokens

Each blockchain will have its own Qchain utility token:
the EQC token for Ethereum, and the XQC token for NEM.

EQC and XQC are the resources used to participate in the Qchain application services. These two tokens are transferred between users to pay for services, e.g. an advertiser renting an ad space on a publisher’s blog.

The goal of EQC and XQC is to cultivate a rich ecosystem around Qchain for all advertiser and publisher marketing needs. We strive for Qchain, EQC, and XQC to become synonymous with a decentralized marketing ecosystem where control has been placed back where it belongs –– in the hands of users.

THE

MARKETPLACE




Distributing capital and control back to the people .

Our unique three-agent model is an equitable approach to ad networking that rewards all parties for acting fairly and in a mutually beneficial manner.

Advertisers

Advertisers create ad content and media resources to be served to their target audiences. In exchange for ad space, Advertisers offer payment in the form of tokens to Publishers. Advertisers can specify where and how their ads are delivered, and how much they want to pay.




Publishers

Publishers agree to display their websites content produced by Advertisers in exchange for token payments. Publishers can specify criteria for content that they are willing to serve, including payment, content type, duration of time, and more.




Host Network

Hosts provide bandwidth and compute capacity in exchange for a percentage of the token payments. Collectively, the Host Network serves as a decentralized delivery network for the Qchain Custom Content Marketplace.




Ethereum & NEM

All transactions between Advertisers and Publishers are executed on the blockchain, ensuring that terms of payment are fulfilled and that transactions are conducted fairly and transparently.

No central authority takes a disproportionately high cut of the transaction.

Advertisers see lower costs and greater control over their content.

Publishers receive higher revenues, without a middle-man taking a huge cut.

Hosts are rewarded for expanding the accessibility and decentralization of the network.
