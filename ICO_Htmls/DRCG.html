What is DRCG?

DRCG Coin is a decentralized cryptocoin token backed by a precious metal called ‘Gold’. Of all the precious metals, gold is the most popular as an investment. Customers generally buy gold as a way of diversifying risks, especially through the use of futures contracts and derivatives.

What cryptocurrencies can I use to purchase?

You can use BTC /ETH /LTC and Paypal to buy DRCG Token.

How can I participate in the ICO Token sale?

To participate in pre-sale create an account with DRCG and make purchases. Go to www.drcgold.io

How do I benefit from the ICO Token?

Participate in the Crowdsale, buy token and hold till the development of DRCG Gold mining project establish.  Since the Coin will appreciate in value over time, you will be able to sell the coin to individuals or to the exchanges for a profit. You also will be able to pay for services using your DRCG Coin. You will also be able to purchase gold from the DRCG project or sell back your tokens.

Where can I see the business plan?

You can find a business plan on our website in a document, called white paper: white paper. Also, you can visit any of our offices to see the plan with the help of our staff.

When can I purchase my tokens and send them to my Ethereum wallet?

You will be able to purchase your tokens in the start of ICO. Until the tokens are sent into your Ethereum wallet,  you will be able to see your coins in your personal account that you registered the first time you joined the system.

I’ve invested, but my tokens don’t appear

Your tokens will arrive in or after 24 hours

I can’t register, I didn’t get a confirmation e-mail

Check your spam folder and also make sure that you’re checking the right email account

I’ve requested a withdrawal from my personal account to my own wallet, but nothing happens, what can I do?

When ICO starts, you will be able to withdraw your tokens. Tokens will not be withdrawn until the conclusion of the ICO.

Where can I find status of my payment?

You can find the status of your payments in your personal account in the “Buy ICO tokens” section

Is there a minimum/maximum purchase?

The minimum purchase may be due to the limitation of the Ethereum platform - wei (1/2500 ETH). Also, the minimum purchase set by us during Pre-ICO can be found on the form when you are ready to purchase the tokens. Since this amount is subject to change, please contact customer service for confirmation. The maximum purchase depends on the amount of available tokens at the moment.

Why did my transaction fail?

It is difficult to answer without transaction ID. You can write to us and we will try to solve the problem. Make sure to send the correct amount of payment.

How can I check the status of my transaction?

All information will be displayed in your web-based wallet MyEtherWallet at MYETHERWALLET.COM. If your balance hasn’t changed you can check the status of your transaction by entering your transaction ID on the service of ETH blockchain (such as etherscan.io and etherchain.org).

What exchanges will DRCG be listed on? And when?

DRCG tokens will be listed in 2018 after the ICO. Two exchanges have already been confirmed, more exchanges are still being negotiated. All information about the cryptocurrency exchanges will be placed on our website and official sources.

Will there be any follow-on CO with additional DRCG tokens emission?

At this point, there are no plans for follow-on CO. If the follow-on CO takes place, we will inform all our customers beforehand and not later than 2 weeks before the start of coin offering.
