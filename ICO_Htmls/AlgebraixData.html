Revolutionizing Rewards

ASK is a currency that can be earned, traded and spent at the time and location of your choice. Its utility extends beyond the ordinary rewards program. Businesses can instantly tap into the power of permission by using ASK to engage and reward customers.

A use case for ASK

Our flagship app where users opt-in to register their personal interests and other key data, and embark on various shopping and entertainment journeys. Users earn ASK for engaging with videos, surveys, and other content that addresses their needs and desires. Users can spend ASK and other leading currencies on the Permission platform.

An optimized ecosystem

Permission’s SDK will allow devs to build apps that enable businesses to become permission marketers and meet consumers’ increasing (and rightful!) demand for rewards. The growth of permission-based applications will incentivize the currency’s growth and enhance its compounding effect.
