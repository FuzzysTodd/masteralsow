About us

For over two years, we have been focused on building Lisk, a blockchain application platform. Our work involves researching state-of-the-art cryptography, developing consensus protocols and designing intuitive user interfaces.

Mission

We’re on a mission to build a robust and accessible ecosystem of developer tools and user interfaces for Lisk.

Vision

In a market that is full of unclarity, we're building trust for people who want to extend their field of work to blockchain.

Principle

Make things matter . In order to have a real impact in the world, we see beyond the idealism of blockchain.
